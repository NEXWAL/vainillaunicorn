Introduction

Fear, an emotion or rather a reaction that has accompanied the human since he is capable of feeling and reasoning. The same human, for years, has been creating different ways of entertainment to create that terrifying sensation that we often like so much... That discomfort, that insecurity, that adrenaline, obviously that we are addicted to that fleeting sensation and I want to take advantage of it with my project. Belgor Wormo.
